Methodolgy

Although I can and have used Bootstrap and Foundation frameworks with the jQuery library to create websiets. I prefer to use my own Gulp powered build system using the Bourbon & Neat Sass libraries with vanilla Javascript.

I am the process of mastering React / Redux & Webpack.

Web Apps

MyFootyTeam.net

My first attempt at the MVC pattern in Javascript. This app allows a user to create their own Essendon footy squad with average team statistics generated as each player is added. A PNG of the squad can then be exported.

Simon Game

Do you remember the classic Simon game? I don't! But as the final project of the Free Code Camp Front End Development Certification I made an online version. Using the React libary I made a concerted effort to replicate the behaviour of the real thing.

About Me

My name is Thomas Bullock and I am an aspiring front-end developer. For the last 10 years I've been working with electronics in the manufacturing industry making and repairing various medical, military and financial devices. Some of the companies I've worked for include Cochlear Ltd, Micreo Ltd and Quest Payment Systems.

Throughout this period as an active musician I've been casually involved in various design activities such as websites, email templates, CD/Vinyl artwork and gig posters. Two years ago I discovered Team Treehouse and for the first time 'lifted the hood' on websites and began to learn how they worked. Within a year my ever expanding desire to learn more lead me to quit my job and pursue my studies full time.

This website features a selection of my web creations from the last 12 months.
